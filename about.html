About BILD

A not-for-profit community for people of British Indian heritage living in the UAE.

Who we are

BILD (British Indians Living in Dubai UAE) is a not-for-profit community created exclusively for people of British Indian heritage—or those married to someone who is—now living in the UAE. We are centered on the shared cultural identity and lived experience of being British Indian, and we welcome all religions and faiths without discrimination.

We recognise the diversity across the wider South Asian community and appreciate the many vibrant groups in the UAE. BILD exists solely to provide a secure space for the unique British Indian voice and experience to thrive abroad.

What we do

We connect members through cultural celebrations (e.g., Diwali), social meet-ups, family-friendly activities, and opportunities to support and grow British Indian businesses, professionals, and entrepreneurs across the UAE.

Members are encouraged to invite other British Indians—each new member strengthens the network and the support we can offer one another.

Our values

  • Safe & kind: A welcoming environment with zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination.
  • Community first: We help one another connect, grow, and succeed.
  • Respect: We celebrate shared heritage while embracing individual differences.
